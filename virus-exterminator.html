Overview

Virus Exterminator is a top-down shoot â€™em up game created with Stencyl. The player takes the role of a lone fighter tasked with eliminating waves of virus-like enemies that threaten humanity. Designed as a fast-paced arcade experience, the game focuses on reflexes, strategy, and survival.

Core Gameplay

The player controls a ship/character that moves freely across the screen, shooting projectiles to destroy incoming viruses. Each wave becomes increasingly challenging, requiring careful dodging and precise aiming.

  • Move in all directions with smooth controls
  • Shoot to eliminate virus enemies
  • Survive as long as possible while avoiding damage
  • Progressive difficulty with tougher waves

Features & Challenges

  • Multiple virus enemy types with different behaviors
  • Fast-paced gameplay loop inspired by classic arcade shooters
  • Balancing player movement and shooting for smooth gameplay
  • Level design focused on wave progression and difficulty scaling

Development Reflection

Developing Virus Exterminator helped me practice structuring core gameplay mechanics in Stencyl. The main challenge was balancing enemy spawn rates and player speed to keep the game fun without being overwhelming. Through iteration and testing, I learned how important small adjustments are for overall game feel.
