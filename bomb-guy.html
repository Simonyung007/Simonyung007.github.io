Bomb Guy

Action Platformer | Escape from the Robot Factory

Overview

Bomb Guy is a 2D side-scrolling action platformer created in Stencyl. The story follows a little boy who accidentally stumbles into a hidden robot factory, only to discover itâ€™s filled with hostile machines and dangerous traps. To escape, the player must solve puzzles, dodge deadly hazards, and use bombs strategically to defeat enemies and progress through levels.

Core Gameplay

Players must navigate the factory using precise movement and bomb-based mechanics. Bombs serve as both weapons and tools for solving puzzles, requiring careful timing and positioning. The game combines platforming challenges with combat against various robot enemies.

  • Run, jump, and double jump to traverse tricky levels
  • Create and throw bombs to destroy enemies and obstacles
  • Interact with objects (doors, switches, NPCs) to progress
  • Avoid traps such as spikes, saws, fire, and conveyor belts

Controls

  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Jump: W or Spacebar
  • Double Jump: W + W
  • Interact: R
  • Create Bomb: J
  • Throw Bomb: Hold J and Release

Enemies & Hazards

  • Spider Bomb: Wanders within a set area
  • Drone: Drops atomic bombs when triggered
  • Shooter: Fires bullets when the player is in range
  • Boss: Blocks the final stage and must be defeated to escape
  • Traps: Spikes, hammers, fire machines, conveyor belts, mimic chests, and more
